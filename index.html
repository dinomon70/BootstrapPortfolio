About Me

Hello. My name is Dean. I grew up in a small ranching town in Central North Dakota. At the tender age of 16, I moved to Salt Lake City, UT. I graduated High School in 1988. I spent the next 3 years playing football for the University of North Dakota. My senior year I transferred to the University of Arizona. I graduated with a B.S. in Business Administration.

In 2010, I had my first midlife crisis. I uprooted my family to the Oregon coast so I could attend Oregon State University - Go Beavs!!! There began my journey into the craft brew industry. I am currently the Production Manager for Utah's largest brewery. I'm known through out the industry for building strong production teams.

I currently reside in Herriman, UT with my wife, 3 boys,and 2 labs.
